Ingredients:

    1 (9 inch) graham cracker pie crust
    8 ounces cream cheese, softened
    3/4 cup sugar
    1 tablespoon lemon juice
    1⁄2 teaspoon vanilla
    1 dash salt
    2 eggs

CRUST:
    2 cups finely ground graham cracker crumbs
    8 tablespoons melted butter
    1/4 cup brown sugar
    1/4 teaspoon cinnamon

TOPPING:
    1 cup sour cream
    3 tablespoons sugar
    1⁄2 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:

 1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.

 2. For crust, mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, melted butter, and 
    cinnamon until well blended. Press mixture into a 9 inch pie plate.
    Bake for 7 minutes. Cool.

 3. While crust is baking, combine cream cheese, sugar, lemon juice, 
    vanilla & salt in bowl. Mix until well blended.

 3. Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.

 4. Pour filling in pie crust. Bake 25-35 minutes or until knife 
    inserted comes out clean.

TOPPING:
 1. Combine sour cream, 3 tablespoons sugar and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla 
    in bowl.

 2. Carefully spread over pie.

 3. Continue baking 10 minutes; cool. Chill 3 hours.
